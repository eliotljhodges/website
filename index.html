About

I am a senior studying mathematics at Harvard University. Most of my undergraduate research has been in combinatorics (advised by Joe Gallian and Colin Defant) and number theory (advised by Melanie Matchett Wood and Ashvin Swaminathan). This summer, I will serve as an advisor at the Duluth REU.

More on my research and mathematical interests can be found by visiting the respective links. My curriculum vitae contains an up-to-date list of my publications, preprints, and talks in addition to some details about the extracurricular activities I have been involved with in college.
